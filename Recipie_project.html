Sweet Potatoes with Smokey Yogurt and Chickpeas

Roast your sweet potatoes ahead of time to get this dish on the table superfast come dinner. Or, just nuke 'em in the microwave for a few minutes while you saute the chickpeas.'

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 c plain low-fat yogurt
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced, divided
  • 2 tsp toasted cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp plus 2 tsp olive oil, devided
  • Salt and pepper
  • 115-oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 c toasted pine nuts
  • 4 med baked sweet potatoes, split lenghtwise
  • Chopped fresh dill

STEPS

  • COMBINE yogurt, 2 cloves of garlic, cumin, paprika, 2 teaspoons of olive oil, salt and pepper in bowl and mix well. Set aside.
  • SAUTE chickpeas and remaining 2 cloves of garlic and 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat, until fragrant and warmed through. 3-4 minutes.
  • SPOON a quarter of the yogurt mixtrure into the sweet potatoes. Top with a quarter of chickpeas and 1 tablespoon of pine nuts. Top with dill, serve warm.

Summer Chicken

A great dish for dinner parties and family meals in - quick, easy and very tasty

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 red or yellow pepper
  • 4 tbsp pesto
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

STEPS

  1. Put the chicken breasts in a large, shallow roasting tray and drizzle with olive oil, placing under the grill for 10 minutes.
  2. While the chicken is cooking, roughly chop up the courgette and pepper into large slices
  3. Take the chicken out from under the grill, adding in the vegetables, coating them in the cooking oil.
  4. Place the full tray back under the grill for another 5-10 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Take the tray out from under the grill, drizzle with pesto, and serve.
